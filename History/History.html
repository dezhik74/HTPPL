Device parameters:  
Major radius R = 0.625 m
Minor radius a = 0.20 m
Magnetic field Btor < 1.2 T
Current Ip < 45 kA
Electron density Ne < 2.5 1019m-3
Electron temperature Te< 450 eV
Ion temperature Ti< 150 eV
Additional heating PLHH< 100 kW
  PECRH < 250 kW
